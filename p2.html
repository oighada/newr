Tousism in Italy

With 52.4 million tourists a year (2016), Italy is
the fifth most visited country in international tourism arrivals.
[1] People mainly visit Italy for its rich culture, cuisine, history,
fashion and art, its beautiful coastline and beaches, its mountains,
and priceless ancient monuments. Italy also contains more World Heritage
Sites than any other country in the world. Tourism is one of Italy's fastest
growing and most profitable industrial sectors, with an estimated revenue of €189.1 billion.
